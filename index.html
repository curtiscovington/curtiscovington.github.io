About Curtis

I build reproducible ML experiments and the tools around them—mostly interpretability work on open models, with an emphasis on clear methodology, measurable results, and artifacts you can run.

Lately that has meant sparse autoencoders, feature discovery, and small-model labs that scale into bigger questions. This site is my working notebook: experiments, write-ups, and the occasional engineering note when the implementation is the point.
